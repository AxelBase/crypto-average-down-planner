Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Crypto Average Down Planner, a free, open-source web utility designed to help cryptocurrency investors calculate averaging down strategies. This tool operates entirely in your browser as a stateless static site. We prioritize your privacy above all and adhere to a zero-data-collection policy. No personal information, usage data, or input values are ever collected, stored, or transmitted. This Privacy Policy explains our practices in clear terms to build trust.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The planner is built to function without any data gathering. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal Information: Names, emails, IP addresses, or any identifiers.
  • Input Data: Your entered amounts, averages, or market prices remain strictly on your device.
  • Usage Analytics: No tracking of visits, sessions, browser details, or device information.
  • Cookies or Trackers: The site uses no cookies, local storage persistence, or third-party scripts for monitoring.

All calculations happen client-side using JavaScript, ensuring complete isolation of your data.

3. How the Tool Operates

When you use the Crypto Average Down Planner:

  • Local Processing: Inputs are processed instantly in your browser with pure JavaScript functions.
  • No Transmission: Nothing is sent to servers, CDNs for data, or external services.
  • Temporary Only: Any transient browser memory clears on reset, reload, or closing the tab.

This design makes the tool ideal for sensitive financial planning without privacy risks.

4. Third-Party Involvement

We use Bootstrap via CDN for styling, but it loads static assets only—no tracking. No analytics, ads, or external embeds exist.

5. Security Considerations

Since no data leaves your device, security focuses on your environment. We recommend using updated browsers and secure connections (HTTPS via hosting).

6. Children's Privacy

The tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from minors.

7. International Compliance

Our no-collection approach complies with GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws by default.

8. Policy Updates

Changes will appear here with updated dates. Continued use implies acceptance.

9. Contact

For questions, refer to the project repository or site footer links.

Your privacy is protected by design—no data means no risk.
